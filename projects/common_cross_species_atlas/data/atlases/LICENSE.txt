Common Cross-Species Atlas — Atlas Licensing Information
================================================================================

This directory contains the **minimal necessary atlas files for reproducibility**
of the **Common Cross-Species Atlas (Release 1.0)**.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
File Naming and Provenance
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The official public release atlases are available at:

    https://github.com/frankyeh/data-atlas

and are distributed there as:

    <species>_CHA.nii.gz

The corresponding files included here are named:

    cha_<species>.nii.gz

These files are direct derivatives of the public release and include placeholder
regions for a small set of areas that were omitted from the official release due
to incomplete cross-species correspondence:

    • OLF_Claustrum  
    • BG_Substantia_Nigra  
    • THL_Subthalamus  
    • CBL  
    • BST

The placeholders serve only as approximate anatomical references and are retained
to preserve continuity with intermediate analyses and figure generation.  
However, all current quantitative analyses and visualizations **explicitly exclude**
these regions to remain consistent with the official Release 1.0 dataset.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Source and License Information
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Each atlas is derived from the corresponding species atlas distributed through
the DSI Studio **data-atlas** repository. Users must follow the **license, citation,
and usage terms** specified there:

    https://github.com/frankyeh/data-atlas/<species>/

Examples:
    • https://github.com/frankyeh/data-atlas/mouse/
    • https://github.com/frankyeh/data-atlas/marmoset/
    • https://github.com/frankyeh/data-atlas/rhesus/

Some species provide explicit `license.txt` files, while others are covered under
the general DSI Studio usage policy.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Usage
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

These atlases are provided **solely for research and non-commercial use**.
When publishing or redistributing results derived from these data, please:

1. Cite the **Common Cross-Species Atlas (bioRxiv 2025.09.08.675002)**, and  
2. Acknowledge the **original source atlas and institution** according to the
   license and citation terms provided in the DSI Studio repository.
